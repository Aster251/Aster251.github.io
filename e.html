Vision Statement

Excellence in holistic human resource development of learners towards building a just and humane society.

Mission Statement

Benchmarking academic and personal excellence with value integration and capacity building to transform the young minds, in keeping with the spirit of the Apostolic Carmel Congregation, our School (a Christian Minority Institution) provides a healthy and sound environment for exploring their hidden potential, sparking innovative thinking, developing ecological sensitivity, seeking spiritual aspirations, enhancing emotional competencies and reaching out in compassion to champion the cause of justice, love, truth, harmony and peace in the society.

International School Award

Our School had won the International School Award by British Council in the year 2010. This award is given for 3 years after the school had undertaken a number of Activities, projects, exhibition, field trips related to Global Educational Themes.

We worked for the re-accreditation of this award in the year 2013. The Principal Sr. Melissa A.C and Co-ordinator Mrs. Silvia Benis oriented the staff and students about various activities to be undertaken and all responded positively. All were eager, enthusiastic, and determined to explore various possibilities in conducting various projects, by surfing the net, reading books, interacting with people..
